Built for fast saving & calm browsing

ShareItSaveIt keeps your social media links organized without noise, feeds, or tracking. Just a clean library that’s always ready when you are.

Save anywhere

Save from any social app

Use the iOS Share Sheet to instantly save links from Instagram, TikTok, X, YouTube, Reddit, or any browser. No copy-paste — just tap “Share” → “ShareItSaveIt”.

Organize

Folders that make sense to you

Group saved links by creator, topic, mood, project, or anything else. Create a library that matches how your brain works, not how a feed is designed.

Privacy

Private by design

There are no accounts, no analytics, and no servers. All your bookmarks stay on your device, under your control — not on someone else’s cloud.
