ShareItSaveIt – Support

ShareItSaveIt stores all data locally on your device and does not use servers, analytics, or cloud syncing. If you need help, you can reach out using the information below.

Contact

Email: your-support-email@example.com

When you contact us, please include:

  • Your device model (for example, iPhone 14)
  • iOS version
  • App version
  • A short description of the issue
  • Screenshots or steps to reproduce the problem (if possible)

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

All saved links, folders, and notes are stored only on your device. No data is uploaded, collected, or synced to external servers.

If I delete the app, will my bookmarks be erased?

Yes. Since data is stored locally and not synced anywhere, uninstalling the app removes all saved links.

Can I restore my links after reinstalling the app?

Only if you restore your iPhone from a full device backup made before uninstalling the app. We cannot recover data for you because we do not run servers for this app.

The app crashed or froze. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  • Close and reopen the app.
  • Restart your iPhone.
  • Update to the latest version of iOS.
  • Update ShareItSaveIt to the latest version from the App Store.

If the issue continues, email us with the details so we can investigate.
